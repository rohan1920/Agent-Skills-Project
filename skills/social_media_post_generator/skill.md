# Social Media Post Generator Skill

## Purpose:
Generate social media posts, captions, or story ideas quickly for products or events.

## What it replaces:
Manual creation of posts, captions, or promotional text.

## How it works:
1. Input: Product name + occasion or theme
2. Process: Skill formats content in an attractive, ready-to-post style
3. Output: Short, catchy caption or story idea suitable for social media
