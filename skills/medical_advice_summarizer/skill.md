# Medical Advice Summarizer Skill

## Purpose:
Summarize medical information or articles into simple, easy-to-read notes.

## What it replaces:
Manual reading and summarizing of medical text for students, patients, or healthcare notes.

## How it works:
1. Input: Medical text or article
2. Process: Extract main points such as symptoms, causes, and advice
3. Output: Short, structured summary highlighting the key information
